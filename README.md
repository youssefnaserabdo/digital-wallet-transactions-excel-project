![Overview Dashboard](https://github.com/youssefnaserabdo/digital-wallet-transactions-excel-project/raw/main/digital-wallet-assets/1_overview.png)

This dashboard provides a high-level overview of user transactions, payment behavior, and total financial performance across the digital wallet platform. It includes key KPIs, payment method analysis, location filters, and quarterly trends.

### KPI Summary Cards:

- **Total Transactions >** Represents the total number of completed transactions across all payment methods.
- **Total Paid Amount >** Shows the overall monetary value paid by users on the platform.
- **Total Cashback >** Reflects the total cashback amount issued to users.
- **Top Payment Method >** Highlights the most frequently used payment method among all users.

### This Dashboard also features a variety of interactive visuals, including:

- **Bar Chart:** Distribution of Payment Methods to identify which payment method generated the highest transaction value and understand user preferences.
- **Line Chart:** Quarterly Paid Amount to visualize seasonal trends and identify peak quarters with the highest spending volume.
- **Slicers:** Location Filter to help in comparing behavior across different demographic areas.

The overview dashboard provides an immediate understanding of financial performance, user behavior, and payment trends. It helps stakeholders track total spending, compare payment methods, analyze quarterly shifts, and segment users based on location.

![Insights Dashboard](https://github.com/youssefnaserabdo/digital-wallet-transactions-excel-project/raw/main/digital-wallet-assets/2_insights.png)

This dashboard focuses on user behavior, loyalty points, cashback activity, and device usage. It provides deeper insights that help understand how customers interact with the digital wallet system across different regions and platforms.

### KPI Summary Cards:

- **Successful Rate >** Represents the total number of successful transactions during the analysis period.
- **Total Loyalty Points >** Shows the total accumulated loyalty points earned by users across all transactions.
- **Last Transaction >** Indicates the date of the most recent transaction recorded in the dataset.
- **Active Location >** Reflects the dominant customer location category based on recent activity.

### Interactive visuals include:

- **Area Chart:** Monthly Distribution of Cashback and this area chart visualizes how cashback amounts fluctuate month-by-month from January to December.
- **Treemap:** Loyalty Points by Location and this treemap breaks down the total loyalty points earned by users in each geographical category (Urban - Suburban - Rural).
- **Pie Chart:** Device Type Usage and this pie chart shows the distribution of devices used to access the digital wallet (Android - iOS - Web).
- **Slicers:** This filter allows dynamic segmentation of all charts based on the selected payment method (Bank Transfer - Credit Card - Debit Card - UPI - Wallet Balance).

This dashboard gives a deep understanding of how users interact with the digital wallet systemâ€”not just through spending, but through loyalty programs, cashback claims, preferred devices, and regional activity. ## 3ï¸âƒ£ Performance Dashboard

![Performance Dashboard](https://github.com/youssefnaserabdo/digital-wallet-transactions-excel-project/raw/main/digital-wallet-assets/3_performance.png)

This dashboard focuses on transaction performance from a product and merchant perspective. It highlights top-performing products, best-earning merchants, and high-value cashback activities.

### Interactive visuals include:

- **Bar Chart:** Top 5 Products by Total Paid Amount to identify which products generate the highest revenue and understand user spending behavior across different categories.
- **Area Chart:** Highest Revenue by Merchants to compare merchant performance and identify top contributors to the platform's revenue.
- **Line Chart:** Top 5 Cashbacked Transactions to track major cashback payouts and analyze promotional cost distribution.
- **Pie Chart:** Top 5 Merchants by Total Paid Amount to evaluate merchant dominance and revenue share distribution.
- **Slicers:** Product Category Filter to analyze performance metrics for specific product types and study category-based revenue patterns.

The performance dashboard provides a complete view of the platform's revenue streams by highlighting top products, high-earning merchants, and cashback patterns. It helps identify where customers spend most, which merchants perform best, and which product categories drive the highest financial activity.
